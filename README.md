# 📊 Nigerian Bank Customer Churn Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview
This project analyzes customer churn behavior in a Nigerian bank using Power BI. The goal is to identify key factors driving customer attrition and provide actionable insights to improve customer retention.

Rather than focusing only on dashboard creation, this project emphasizes **business understanding, customer behavior, and decision-making using data**.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Calculate and analyze customer churn rate  
- Identify key drivers of churn  
- Understand customer behavior patterns  
- Provide recommendations to improve retention  

---

## ❓ Key Business Questions
This analysis was guided by the following questions:

- What is the overall churn rate?  
- Which regions have the highest churn?  
- Does account balance influence churn?  
- Which age group churns the most?  
- Do active customers still churn?  
- Does tenure (customer lifespan) affect churn?  
- Does account type influence customer retention?  
- What is the gender distribution of churned customers?  

---

## 📊 Key Metrics
- Total Customers: **600**  
- Churned Customers: **167**  
- Churn Rate: **27.83%**  
- Average Account Balance: **768K**  
- Average Transactions per Month: **15**  

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

### 📉 High Churn Among New Customers
Customers with low tenure (1–3 years) are more likely to churn, indicating poor onboarding or early engagement.

### 💰 Low Activity Drives Churn
Customers with **low account balances and fewer transactions** show a significantly higher likelihood of churning.

### 📍 Regional Variations
Certain regions show higher churn levels, suggesting differences in customer experience or service delivery.

### 📱 Digital Banking Matters
Customers who use digital banking services are less likely to churn compared to non-users.

### 👥 Gender Distribution
Churn exists across both genders, with slight variation in distribution.

### 🏦 Account Type Influence
Savings account holders show slightly higher churn compared to current account holders.

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations
- Improve onboarding for new customers  
- Promote digital banking adoption  
- Target low-activity customers with incentives  
- Focus retention strategies on high-churn regions
